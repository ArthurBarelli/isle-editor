Wikipedia

Wikipedia

The Wikipedia component scans the Wikipedia and returns an article - if anything valid is found under the given address.

Example:

<Wikipedia
    showSearch
    speech
    language = "de-DE"
/>

Open interactive preview

Options:

  • language: specifies the given language. us-EN, de-DE, and fr-FR are supported (English, German, French).
  • invisible: boolean indicating whether the component is invisible or not (invisible makes sense in an environment where speech recognition shall be the sole interface)
